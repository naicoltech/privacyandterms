Privacy Policy

Naicol Tech — Effective date: February 26, 2026

Introduction

Naicol Tech ("we", "us", or "our") develops and publishes mobile applications. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use any of our apps.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information directly. All user data (preferences, progress, settings, and any content you create within our apps) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

Our apps may use the following third-party services that may collect information:

Google AdMob

Some of our apps use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data including:

  • Device advertising identifier
  • Approximate location (IP-based)
  • App interaction data
  • Device information (model, OS version)

This data is used to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

Some of our apps offer in-app purchases. All payment processing is handled entirely by the platform's store (Google Play or Apple App Store). We do not have access to your payment details.

Data Stored on Your Device

Our apps may store data locally on your device, such as:

  • User preferences and settings
  • App progress and activity records
  • Notification preferences

You can delete all this data at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the app.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Data Security

All data transmitted by third-party services is encrypted in transit using HTTPS. Your local data is protected by your device's built-in security features.

Your Choices

  • Remove ads: Where available, you can purchase an ad-free version to stop all advertising and related data collection by AdMob.
  • Reset your data: Clear the app's data through your device settings or uninstall the app.
  • Ad personalization: You can opt out of personalized ads in your device's privacy settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
